Waking Up Adhkar

  1. Home
  2. Dhikr & Du'a
  3. Waking Up

Begin your day with remembrance and gratitude to Allah. These authentic supplications are to be recited upon waking up, following the Sunnah.

Upon Waking

الحمدُ للهِ الذي أحيانا بعدَ ما أماتَنا وإليه النشور

(1x)

All praise is for Allah who gave us life after causing us to die (sleep), and to Him is the return.

Source: Sahih al-Bukhari

Alhamdu lillāh illadhī aḥyānā baʿda mā amātanā wa ilayhin-nushūr.

Reported in Sahih al-Bukhari. Beginning the day with praise renews faith and brings barakah.

Start the morning with gratitude and remembrance, acknowledging the blessing of life and renewing resolve to return to Allah.

Praise on Regaining Health & Breath

الحمدُ للهِ الذي عافاني في جسدي ورَدَّ عليَّ روحي وأَذِنَ لي بذِكرِه

(1x)

All praise is for Allah who granted me well‑being in my body, returned my soul to me, and allowed me to remember Him.

Source: Sunan at‑Tirmidhi

Alhamdu lillāh illadhī ʿāfānī fī jasadī, wa radda ʿalayya rūḥī, wa adhina lī bidhikrih.

Recite as soon as you awaken and sit up. Follow with Morning Adhkar for comprehensive protection and blessings.

Recorded in Sunan at-Tirmidhi; incorporating this dhikr preserves health of heart and tongue upon waking.
