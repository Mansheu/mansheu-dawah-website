Seek Allah’s protection and blessing in travel: at departure, while riding, and on return.

Embarking

سبحانَ الذي سخَّرَ لنا هذا وما كنَّا لهُ مقرنين، وإنَّا إلى ربِّنا لمنقلبون

(When setting off/riding)

Subḥāna alladhī sakhkhara lanā hādhā wa mā kunnā lahu muqrinīn, wa innā ilā rabbinā lamunqalibūn.

Glory be to the One who subjected this to us, and we could not have done so on our own. And surely to our Lord we will return.

Source: Qur'an 43:13–14

Qur’anic remembrance that instills humility and destination awareness.

Say it upon boarding a vehicle or setting off, mindful of Allah’s favor in enabling travel.

Travel Du'a

اللهمَّ إنّا نسألُكَ في سفرِنا هذا البِرَّ والتقوى، ومن العملِ ما ترضى، اللهمَّ هوِّن علينا سفرَنا هذا واطوِ عنَّا بُعدَه، اللهمَّ أنتَ الصاحبُ في السفرِ والخليفةُ في الأهل

(At departure)

Allāhumma innā nasʾaluka fī safarinā hādhā l‑birra wa t‑taqwā, wa mina l‑ʿamali mā tarḍā; Allāhumma hawwin ʿalaynā safaranā hādhā wa‑ṭwi ʿannā buʿdah; Allāhumma anta ṣ‑ṣāḥibu fī s‑safar wa l‑khalīfatu fī l‑ahl.

O Allah, we ask You in this journey for righteousness and God‑consciousness and deeds You approve. O Allah, ease this journey for us and fold up its distance. O Allah, You are the Companion on the journey and Guardian over the family.

Source: Sahih Muslim

Seeks piety, ease, and divine companionship on the journey.

Recite at departure and as needed; complement with adhkar of riding and morning/evening.

On Return

آيِبونَ، تائِبونَ، عابدونَ، لِربِّنا حامدون

(When returning home)

Āʾibūna, tāʾibūna, ʿābidūna, li‑rabbinā ḥāmidūn.

Returning repentant, worshiping, praising our Lord.

Source: Sahih Muslim

Completes the journey with repentance and praise; a sunnah remembrance at return.

Say when approaching one’s city or home as the journey concludes.
