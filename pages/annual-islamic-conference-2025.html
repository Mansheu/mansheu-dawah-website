About This Event

Join us for the highly anticipated Annual Islamic Conference 2025, a three-day gathering that brings together Muslims from across the UK and beyond for spiritual renewal, knowledge sharing, and community building. This year's conference theme, "Strengthening Faith, Building Community, Inspiring Action," reflects our commitment to nurturing both individual spirituality and collective responsibility.

The conference will feature internationally renowned Islamic scholars, engaging workshops, interactive panel discussions, and networking opportunities designed to address the contemporary challenges facing Muslims while celebrating our rich heritage and values. Whether you're seeking spiritual growth, practical guidance, or meaningful connections, this conference offers something for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is childcare available during the conference?

Yes, we offer supervised childcare for ages 3-12 throughout all conference sessions. Please indicate your childcare needs during registration. Limited spots available.

What should I bring to the conference?

Bring your ticket confirmation (printed or digital), notebook for taking notes, and comfortable clothing. Prayer mats will be provided. All conference materials and lunch are included.

Are prayer facilities available?

Yes, dedicated prayer halls for men and women are available throughout the venue with wudu facilities. Prayer times will be announced, and breaks are scheduled accordingly.

Can I attend just one day instead of all three?

While we encourage full attendance for the complete experience, single-day tickets are available for £25 per day. Please contact us directly to arrange this.

Is the conference suitable for non-Muslims?

Absolutely! We welcome people of all faiths who are interested in learning about Islam and engaging in meaningful interfaith dialogue. All sessions are open to everyone.
