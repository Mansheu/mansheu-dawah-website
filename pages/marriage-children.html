Du'as for marriage, love and mercy between spouses, and for righteous children.

Congratulations for Newlyweds

باركَ اللهُ لكَ، وباركَ عليكَ، وجمعَ بينكما في خير

(When congratulating)

Bārakallāhu laka, wa bāraka ʿalayka, wa jamaʿa baynakumā fī khayr.

May Allah bless you, shower blessings upon you, and unite you both in goodness.

Source: Sunan Abi Dawud

Prophetic congratulation that invokes lasting blessing and goodness.

Said to the couple after nikāḥ; avoid jahiliyyah phrases like “bi r‑rifāʾi wa l‑banīn”.

When Marrying a Spouse

اللهمَّ إنّي أسألُكَ خيرَها وخيرَ ما فَطَرتَها عليه، وأعوذُ بكَ من شرِّها وشرِّ ما فَطَرتَها عليه

(Upon marriage)

Allāhumma innī asʾaluka khayrahā wa khayra mā faṭartahā ʿalayh, wa aʿūdhu bika min sharrihā wa sharri mā faṭartahā ʿalayh.

O Allah, I ask You for her goodness and the goodness You created in her nature, and I seek refuge in You from her evil and the evil You created in her nature.

Source: Sunan Abi Dawud

Instills optimism and protection as the new life together begins.

Recite when first approaching one’s spouse; begin married life with remembrance and dua.

For Righteous Offspring

رَبَّنَا هَبْ لَنَا مِنْ أَزْوَاجِنَا وَذُرِّيَّاتِنَا قُرَّةَ أَعْيُنٍ وَاجْعَلْنَا لِلْمُتَّقِينَ إِمَامًا

(Regularly)

Rabbana hab lanā min azwājinā wa dhurriyyātinā qurrata aʿyun, wajʿalnā lil‑muttaqīna imāmā.

Our Lord, grant us from our spouses and our offspring comfort to our eyes, and make us leaders for the righteous.

Source: Qur'an 25:74

A Qur’anic aspiration for righteous families who lead by example.

Incorporate into daily prayers; ask for piety, harmony and beneficial leadership.
