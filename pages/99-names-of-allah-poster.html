About This Printable

The 99 Names of Allah Poster showcases every blessed name with Arabic calligraphy, English transliteration, and a concise meaning. The Design Team layered geometric star patterns and subtle watercolor textures to create a timeless piece that complements modern and traditional interiors alike.

Each name is grouped by colour families to help learners memorise in manageable sets. The layout maintains generous spacing so the Arabic text is clear even when the poster is framed or mounted behind glass. We also included pronunciation marks to support families teaching young children or new Muslims.

Use the full-colour version for statement decor or switch to the minimalist black-and-white edition for ink-friendly classroom prints. Either option transforms any wall into a meaningful reminder of Allah's perfect attributes.

Printing Tips

Optimize Your Print

  • Use 200gsm satin or matte photo paper for gallery-style results; standard 120gsm works for classroom displays.
  • Select "Best" or "High Quality" in printer settings to keep the intricate patterns crisp.
  • Print without scaling (100%) to preserve margins for framing; trim along crop marks if desired.
  • Laminating adds durability for mosque walls and protects from little fingerprints.
  • Eco tip: choose the monochrome version when printing multiple copies or use recycled cardstock.

How to Use

  1. Download the PDF and PNG files and decide which colourway suits your space.
  2. Print on high-quality paper or send the file to a local print shop for large-batch needs.
  3. Mount in a frame or use poster putty to display the Names at eye level.
  4. Pick a handful of Names each week to study, recite aloud, and discuss with family.
  5. Invite guests to point to their favourite Names and share reflections for added barakah.
