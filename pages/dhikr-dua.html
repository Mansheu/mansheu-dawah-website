Morning Adhkar أذكار الصباح

Begin your day with these blessed remembrances

Seeking Allah's Protection

أَعُوذُ بِاللهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ

A'udhu billahi min ash-shaytani'r-rajim
I seek refuge in Allah from Satan, the accursed one.
3 times Quran & Sunnah

Ayat al-Kursi

اللَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ ۚ لَا تَأْخُذُهُ سِنَةٌ وَلَا نَوْمٌ ۚ لَّهُ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ...

Allahu la ilaha illa huwa, al-hayyu al-qayyum. La ta'khudhuhu sinatun wa la nawm...
Allah - there is no deity except Him, the Ever-Living, the Self-Sustaining. Neither drowsiness overtakes Him nor sleep...
Once Quran 2:255

Morning Protection Du'a

أَصْبَحْنَا وَأَصْبَحَ الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ، اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ خَيْرَ هَذَا الْيَوْمِ

Asbahna wa asbahal mulku lillahi rabbil alameen. Allahumma inni as'aluka khayra hadha'l yawm
We have reached the morning and at this very time Allah's kingdom. O Allah, I ask You for the good of this day...
Once Authentic Hadith

Evening Adhkar أذكار المساء

End your day with peaceful remembrance of Allah

Evening Gratitude

أَمْسَيْنَا وَأَمْسَى الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ، اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ خَيْرَ هَذِهِ اللَّيْلَةِ

Amsayna wa amsal mulku lillahi rabbil alameen. Allahumma inni as'aluka khayra hadhihi'l laylah
We have reached the evening and at this very time Allah's kingdom. O Allah, I ask You for the good of this night...
Once Authentic Hadith

Seeking Allah's Forgiveness

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللهَ الَّذِي لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ وَأَتُوبُ إِلَيْهِ

Astaghfirullaha'l-ladhi la ilaha illa huwa'l-hayyu'l-qayyum wa atubu ilayh
I seek forgiveness from Allah, besides whom there is no deity, the Ever-Living, the Self-Sustaining, and I repent to Him.
3 times Prophetic Tradition

Quranic Du'as دعوات قرآنية

Beautiful supplications directly from the Holy Quran

Du'a for Guidance

رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ

Rabbana atina fi'd-dunya hasanatan wa fi'l-akhirati hasanatan wa qina 'adhab an-nar
Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the next world, and save us from the punishment of the Fire.
As needed Quran 2:201

Du'a for Knowledge

رَبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا

Rabbi zidni 'ilma
My Lord, increase me in knowledge.
As needed Quran 20:114

Du'a for Health and Well-being

اللَّهُمَّ أَصْلِحْ لِي دِينِي الَّذِي هُوَ عِصْمَةُ أَمْرِي، وَأَصْلِحْ لِي دُنْيَايَ الَّتِي فِيهَا مَعَاشِي

Allahumma aslih li dini'l-ladhi huwa 'ismatu amri, wa aslih li dunyaya'l-lati fiha ma'ashi
O Allah, set right for me my religion which is the safeguard of my affairs, and set right for me my worldly life wherein is my livelihood...
As needed Sahih Muslim
