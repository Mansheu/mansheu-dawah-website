Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and programs.

Do you offer online courses?

We are currently focused on providing articles and dhikr and du'a collections through the website. Structured online courses are not available yet, but we plan to introduce them in the future, in shāʼ Allāh. You can use the "Courses & Education" subject in the contact form to share your interest or ask about upcoming programs.

How can I contribute articles or resources?

We welcome beneficial, well-researched contributions. Please contact us through this form, select the most relevant subject (such as "Courses & Education" or "Other"), and include "Article Submission" or "Resource Contribution" in your message. Share a brief outline of your proposed content and your background, and we will review and respond as time allows.

Do you collaborate with mosques or educational institutions?

Yes, we are happy to support mosques, Islamic centers, schools, and study circles by sharing our digital resources and exploring possible collaborations. Please contact us with details about your institution and how you would like to use Mansheu Dawah materials, using the "Partnership" subject in the contact form.

How can I report technical issues?

For technical support, please use the "Technical Support" option in the subject field above. Include details about the issue, the page you were on, and your device/browser information so we can resolve it more quickly.

Is all content on Mansheu Dawah free to access?

Yes, all articles, dhikr and du'a collections, and digital tools on our website are free to access. If we introduce any paid offerings in the future, they will be clearly labeled.

Can I download or print your adhkar and articles?

You may download or print our content for personal use, teaching, or dawah, as long as you do not alter it in a misleading way and you keep a clear reference to Mansheu Dawah as the source.

Can I use your materials in my classes or khutbahs?

Yes, you are welcome to use our materials in study circles, school lessons, or khutbahs. Please mention Mansheu Dawah as the source and, where possible, link back to the original page.

How often is the website updated with new content?

We aim to add new articles, reminders, and resources regularly. You can check back often or follow our channels to stay informed about updates.

Do I need an account to use the website?

No, you do not need to create an account to read articles, view adhkar, or use the digital counter. All main features are available without logging in.

Is there a mobile app for Mansheu Dawah?

We do not currently have a mobile app, but the website is designed to be mobile-friendly so you can easily access content from your phone or tablet.
