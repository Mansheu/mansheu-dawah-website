Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and programs.

How can I access your online courses?

You can access our online courses through the Resources section. Simply create an account, browse our course catalog, and enroll in the courses that interest you. All courses are designed to be self-paced and accessible on any device.

Are your publications available in multiple languages?

Yes, we offer publications in multiple languages including English, Arabic, and other regional languages. Each publication page indicates the available language options.

How can I contribute articles to your website?

We welcome scholarly contributions! Please contact us through this form with "Article Submission" as the subject. Include your credentials and a brief outline of your proposed article. Our editorial team will review and respond within 5-7 business days.

Do you offer group discounts for educational institutions?

Yes, we provide special pricing for educational institutions, mosques, and Islamic centers. Contact us with details about your institution and the number of users to receive a customized quote.

How can I report technical issues?

For technical support, please use the "Technical Support" option in the subject field above, or email us directly at support@mansheudawah.org. Include details about the issue and your device/browser information for faster resolution.
