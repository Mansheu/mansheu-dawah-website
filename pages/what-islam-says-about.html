What Islam Says About...

Explore clear, authentic Islamic perspectives on various aspects of life, faith, and society. Each topic is carefully explained using guidance from the Quran and Sunnah.

Basics of Islam

Islam is a monotheistic faith founded on the belief in one God (Allah) and the teachings of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

Explore

Hajj and Umrah

Hajj is the annual pilgrimage to Mecca that every able-bodied Muslim must perform at least once in their lifetime.

Explore

Ramadan

Ramadan is the ninth month of the Islamic calendar when Muslims fast from dawn to sunset. It's a time of spiritual reflection and prayer.

Explore

Shariah

Shariah is Islamic law derived from the Quran and Sunnah, providing guidance on worship, ethics, family life, and social justice.

Explore

Health & Wellness

Islam teaches that the body is a trust from Allah and must be cared for through balanced nutrition, exercise, and cleanliness.

Explore

Charity

Charity is a fundamental principle in Islam. Zakat is one of the Five Pillars, while Sadaqah is highly encouraged.

Explore

Peace & Violence

Islam promotes peace, justice, and the sanctity of human life. The religion condemns terrorism and unjust violence.

Explore

Women

Islam grants women rights to education, property ownership, and spiritual equality. Islamic teachings emphasize respect and dignity for women.

Explore

Men

Men in Islam are called to be protectors and maintainers of their families with justice, compassion, and responsibility.
