Adhan & Masjid

  1. Home
  2. Dhikr & Du'a
  3. Adhan & Masjid

Respond to the Adhan, send blessings on the Prophet, and ask for al‑Wasilah. Enter the masjid with humility and leave seeking Allah’s bounty.

After the Adhan

اللهمَّ ربَّ هذه الدعوةِ التامَّة، والصلاةِ القائمة، آتِ محمّدًا الوسيلةَ والفضيلة، وابعثه مقامًا محمودًا الذي وعدتَه

(After repeating the Adhan)

O Allah, Lord of this perfect call and the established prayer, grant Muhammad the wasilah and virtue, and raise him to the praised station You have promised.

Source: Sahih al‑Bukhari

Allāhumma rabba hādhihi d‑daʿwati t‑tāmmah, wa ṣ‑ṣalāti l‑qāʾimah, ātī Muḥammadan al‑wasīlata wa l‑faḍīlah, wabʿath‑hu maqāman maḥmūdan alladhī waʿadtah.

Whoever says it earns the Prophet’s intercession, by Allah’s permission.

First repeat the words of the adhan with the mu’adhdhin; then send ṣalāh upon the Prophet; then recite this du’a.

Entering and Leaving the Masjid

بِسْمِ اللهِ، والصلاةُ والسلامُ على رسولِ الله. اللهمَّ افتح لي أبوابَ رحمتِك

(On entering)

بِسْمِ اللهِ، والصلاةُ والسلامُ على رسولِ الله. اللهمَّ إني أسألُكَ من فضلِكَ

(On leaving)

In the Name of Allah, and peace and blessings be upon the Messenger of Allah. O Allah, open for me the doors of Your mercy. [Leaving:] In the Name of Allah... O Allah, I ask You of Your bounty.

Source: Sahih Muslim

Bismillāh, waṣ‑ṣalātu wa s‑salāmu ʿalā Rasūlillāh. Allāhumma iftaḥ lī abwāba raḥmatik. [Leaving:] Bismillāh, waṣ‑ṣalātu wa s‑salāmu ʿalā Rasūlillāh. Allāhumma innī asʾaluka min faḍlik.

Entering with mercy and leaving seeking bounty encapsulate the mosque’s purpose: purification and provision.

Enter with the right foot and pray two rakaʿāt of greeting the masjid (tahiyyat al‑masjid) if not at a prohibited time.
