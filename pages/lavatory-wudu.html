Lavatory & Wudu

  1. Home
  2. Dhikr & Du'a
  3. Lavatory & Wudu

Duas when entering and leaving the lavatory, and remembrance after wudu — preserving purity inwardly and outwardly.

Entering the Lavatory

بِسْمِ اللهِ، اللهمَّ إني أعوذُ بكَ من الخُبُثِ والخبائث

(Before entering)

In the Name of Allah. O Allah, I seek refuge in You from male and female devils.

Source: Sahih al‑Bukhari, Sahih Muslim

Bismillāh, Allāhumma innī aʿūdhu bika mina-l‑khubuthi wal‑khabāʾith.

Protects one from harm and impurity while entering an unclean place, following the Prophet’s guidance.

Enter with the left foot; avoid mentioning Allah’s name inside; leave with the right foot and say the du’a below.

Leaving the Lavatory

غُفْرَانَكَ

(After exiting)

“Your forgiveness.” Short and profound — seeking pardon for any lapse while occupied.

Source: Sunan Abi Dawud, at‑Tirmidhi

Ghufrānak.

Maintains remembrance even after mundane tasks; a consistent path to forgiveness.

Exit with the right foot and say this du’a immediately upon leaving.

After Wudu

أشهدُ أن لا إلهَ إلا اللهُ وحدهُ لا شريكَ لهُ، وأشهدُ أنَّ محمدًا عبدُهُ ورسولُهُ. اللهمَّ اجعلني من التوابين واجعلني من المتطهرين

(After completing wudu)

I bear witness there is no deity but Allah alone with no partner; and I bear witness Muhammad is His servant and messenger. O Allah, make me of those who repent often and of those who purify themselves.

Source: Sahih Muslim; Sunan at‑Tirmidhi

Ashhadu an lā ilāha illā Allāhu waḥdahu lā sharīka lah, wa ashhadu anna Muḥammadan ʿabduhū wa rasūluh. Allāhumma ijʿalnī mina-t‑tawwābīn wajʿalnī mina-l‑mutaṭahhirīn.

Whoever says it after wudu has the gates of Paradise opened to him; it perfects purification inwardly and outwardly.

Recite immediately upon finishing wudu while facing away from the restroom; then pray two rakʿahs if possible.
