Food & Drink

  1. Home
  2. Dhikr & Du'a
  3. Food & Drink

Begin meals with Allah’s name, end with praise, and follow the etiquettes of eating and drinking.

Before Eating

بِسْمِ الله

(At start)

Bismillāh.

Say: بسم اللهِ في أوَّلهِ وآخرِه — “In the Name of Allah at its beginning and its end.”

Source: Sunan Abi Dawud; at‑Tirmidhi

Mentioning Allah’s name brings barakah to the food and prevents shayṭān from partaking.

Eat with the right hand, from what is nearest, and avoid wastefulness. If you forget Bismillah, say the completion phrase when you remember.

After Eating

الحمدُ للهِ الذي أطعمَني هذا ورزقنيهِ من غيرِ حولٍ مني ولا قوَّة

(After finishing)

Alhamdu lillāh illadhī aṭʿamanī hādhā wa razaqanīhi min ghayri ḥawlin minnī wa lā quwwah.

All praise is for Allah who fed me this and provided it for me without any power or might from myself.

Source: Sunan at‑Tirmidhi

Gratitude upon completion increases blessings and instills humility.

Wipe and lick fingers before washing; avoid criticizing food; praise Allah for His favor.
