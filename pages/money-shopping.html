Seek halal provision and remember Allah in marketplaces where heedlessness is common.

Entering the Market

لا إلهَ إلا اللهُ وحدهُ لا شريكَ لهُ، لهُ المُلْكُ ولهُ الحمدُ، يُحيي ويُمِيتُ، وهوَ حيٌّ لا يموتُ، بيدِهِ الخيرُ وهوَ على كلِّ شيءٍ قدير

(Once)

Lā ilāha illā Allāhu waḥdahu lā sharīka lah, lahul‑mulku wa lahul‑ḥamdu, yuḥyī wa yumīt, wa huwa ḥayyul lā yamūt; biyadihil‑khayr wa huwa ʿalā kulli shay’in qadīr.

There is no deity but Allah alone, no partner has He; His is the dominion and to Him is all praise; He gives life and causes death, and He is Ever‑Living, never dies; in His Hand is all good and He is over all things capable.

Source: Reported in Sunan; virtues mentioned by scholars

Remembrance in busy marketplaces earns immense reward and guards the tongue and heart.

Recite on entering; deal honestly; avoid deception and extravagance.

Halal Sufficiency

اللهمَّ اكْفِني بحلالِكَ عن حرامِكَ، وأغنِني بفضلِكَ عمَّن سِواكَ

(Regularly)

Allāhumma ikfinī bi‑ḥalālika ʿan ḥarāmik, wa aghninī bifaḍlika ʿamman siwāk.

O Allah, suffice me with what You have made lawful against what You have made unlawful, and make me independent by Your favor from all besides You.

Source: Sunan at‑Tirmidhi

Asks for sufficiency in lawful means and independence from creation.

Make this du’a regularly in work and trade; pursue halal and avoid doubtful matters.
