Nightmares

If you see a bad dream, turn slightly to your left and spit lightly three times, seek refuge in Allah from Shayṭān and from the evil of what you saw, do not speak of it to others, and change your sleeping side.

Protection from Bad Dreams

أعوذُ باللهِ منَ الشيطانِ الرجيم، اللهمَّ إني أعوذُ بكَ من شرِّ هذه الرؤيا

(3x spitting light to the left, then 1x du'a)

I seek refuge in Allah from Satan, the expelled. O Allah, I seek Your protection from the evil of this dream.

Source: Sahih Muslim; Adab of dreams

Aʿūdhu billāhi mina sh‑shayṭāni r‑rajīm, Allāhumma innī aʿūdhu bika min sharri hādhihi r‑ruʾyā.

Following the prophetic etiquette protects from the harm of disturbing dreams and removes their effect.

Source: Sahih Muslim

Do not recount the bad dream to anyone; spit lightly to the left three times, change sides, and seek refuge. If needed, pray.
