About This Event

Ramadan evenings at Mansheu Dawah bring together hundreds of worshippers for carefully paced Taraweeh led by our resident and visiting huffaz. The Masjid Committee has mapped every night so families know when to arrive, how to secure iftar seating, and when qiyam will begin. Dedicated volunteers coordinate childcare rooms, parking stewards, and nightly khatm reflections to make the experience serene for everyone.

This year's programme features a full khatm by the 27th night, extended supplications on Fridays, and women-led halaqat in Studio B while Taraweeh continues in the main hall. Community iftars run Mondays through Thursdays with sponsored nights open for booking. The schedule also highlights nights where Qur'an students will recite selected passages before the opening takbir, giving the youth a memorable platform.

Families are encouraged to register for qiyam pods during the last ten nights so we can manage overnight capacity, refreshments, and safety stewards. Eid prayer briefings and zakat-ul-fitr distribution plans are also built into the calendar so you can prepare ahead of the rush.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to register for nightly Taraweeh?

General Taraweeh seating remains first-come, first-served. We only require RSVP for overnight qiyam pods and dedicated family iftar tables.

Will childcare be available?

A supervised quiet room for ages 3-8 runs 8:15 PM - 10:15 PM nightly except Fridays. Spaces are capped at 25 children; sign-up occurs at the welcome desk.

How are the last ten nights managed?

Security wristbands are issued to pod registrants, and separate women's qiyam spaces open on level two. Additional cleaning teams rotate to keep wudu areas refreshed.

Can I sponsor an iftar?

Yes. Email masjidcommittee@mansheudawah.com to sponsor a night. Suggested sponsorship ranges from GBP 750 to 1,200 depending on attendance.

Will Eid prayer also be held here?

Eid prayer will take place at the Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park. Registration links will be announced during the final Taraweeh and via our mailing list.
