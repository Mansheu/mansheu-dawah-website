About This Printable

Our Daily Prayer Tracker is designed to help you maintain consistency in performing your five daily prayers (Salah). This simple yet effective tracking sheet allows you to check off each prayer as you complete it, helping you visualize your progress and build lasting habits of regular worship.

The tracker features a clean, minimalist design with space for one month of prayer tracking. Each day includes checkboxes for Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha prayers, along with an optional notes section where you can record reflections, dua requests, or prayer times.

Whether you're working on establishing your prayer routine, teaching children about the importance of salah, or simply want a visual reminder of your worship consistency, this tracker serves as both a practical tool and a source of motivation.

What's Included

  • 1-page PDF tracker - covers one full month (30/31 days)
  • Checkboxes for all 5 daily prayers - Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha
  • Notes section for personal reflections and prayer times
  • Two versions included: Full color and printer-friendly black & white
  • Compatible with A4 and US Letter paper sizes
  • High-resolution PDF for crisp printing

Printing Tips

How to Get the Best Results

  • Paper Type: Use standard 80-100gsm printer paper for everyday use, or upgrade to cardstock (200-250gsm) for a more durable tracker.
  • Color vs Black & White: The color version is perfect for those who enjoy vibrant designs. The black & white version is ink-friendly and equally effective.
  • Print Settings: Select "Actual Size" or "100% scale" in your printer settings to ensure the layout fits properly on the page.
  • Laminating: For repeated monthly use, consider laminating the tracker and using a dry-erase marker to check off prayers, then wiping clean at month's end.
  • Multiple Months: Print several copies at once to have trackers ready for upcoming months.
  • Eco-Friendly Option: Print on the back of scrap paper or use the black & white version to save ink.

How to Use This Tracker

  1. Download and print the tracker at the beginning of each month (or Islamic month).
  2. Fill in the dates in the provided spaces at the top of each row.
  3. Check off each prayer immediately after completing it - this creates a sense of accomplishment and helps build consistency.
  4. Use the notes section to record the time you prayed, special duas you made, or personal reflections.
  5. Review weekly to see patterns and identify which prayers you might be missing most often.
  6. Celebrate progress - even one more prayer than last month is growth worth acknowledging!
