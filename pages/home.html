Duas for entering and leaving home, invoking Allah’s name and protection, and spreading peace within the household.

Entering the Home

بِسْمِ اللهِ، وَلَجْنَا، وَبِسْمِ اللهِ خَرَجْنَا، وَعَلَى اللهِ رَبِّنَا تَوَكَّلْنَا

(When entering)

In the Name of Allah we enter, and in the Name of Allah we leave, and upon Allah, our Lord, we rely.

Source: Abu Dawud; say salam to family when entering.

Bismillāh, walajnā, wa bismillāhi kharajnā, wa ʿalā Allāhi rabbinā tawakkalnā.

Brings barakah to the household and repels shayṭān when Allah’s name and salām are mentioned.

Enter with “Bismillāh” and greet those inside with salām to spread peace and remembrance.

Leaving the Home

بِسْمِ اللهِ، تَوَكَّلْتُ عَلَى اللهِ، لَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِالله

(When leaving)

In the Name of Allah. I place my trust in Allah. There is no power nor might except with Allah.

Source: Sunan at‑Tirmidhi; protection and guidance are granted.

Bismillāh, tawakkaltu ʿalā Allāh, lā ḥawla wa lā quwwata illā billāh.

One is told: “You are guided, defended and protected,” and shayṭān is kept away.

Renew reliance upon Allah for the day’s affairs when stepping out of the home.
